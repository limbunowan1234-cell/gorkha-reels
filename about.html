About GorkhaReels

The platform for Gorkha creators and community

🎬 Our Mission

GorkhaReels is a community-first short-form video platform designed to empower Gorkha creators, celebrate Himalayan culture, and amplify local voices from Darjeeling and beyond.

We believe in creating a space where creators can express themselves freely, connect with their community, and build sustainable audiences—all while celebrating the rich culture, traditions, and diversity of the Gorkha people.

🌟 Our Vision

To become the leading platform for South Asian creators, where local content thrives and community values are paramount. We envision a future where Gorkha creators compete on the global stage while staying rooted in their culture.

🎯 What We Offer

  • 📹 Creator Tools - Easy upload, editing, and discovery features
  • 💰 Monetization - Ad revenue, tips, sponsorships, and partnerships
  • 🤝 Community - Direct connection with audiences and fellow creators
  • 🌍 Cultural Pride - Features that celebrate Gorkha identity and heritage
  • 📊 Analytics - Real-time insights into your content performance
  • 🔒 Safety First - Community guidelines and moderation for a safe space

✨ Why Choose GorkhaReels?

  • Community-Focused: Built BY creators FOR creators
  • Fast & Responsive: Optimized for mobile-first experience
  • Fair Monetization: Transparent earnings system with timely payouts
  • Cultural Respect: Dedicated to promoting Gorkha and Himalayan content
  • Growing Platform: Join a rapidly expanding community of creators
