Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At GorkhaReels, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using GorkhaReels, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, username, and password (stored securely in encrypted form).

Content You Upload

We store the videos, captions, hashtags, music selections, and any other content you choose to upload or share on the platform.

Profile Details

Information you add to your profile such as your bio, profile picture, and category.

Payment Information

If you set up payouts, we collect bank account or UPI details needed to process your earnings. Payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers.

Usage Data

We automatically collect data about how you interact with the platform, including videos watched, likes, comments, follows, and general activity patterns.

Device & Technical Data

Device type, browser, approximate location (city/region), and basic technical information needed to deliver and improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display and distribute your content to viewers
  • Personalize your feed and recommendations
  • Process payments, tips, and creator earnings
  • Send notifications (e.g., when you're tagged or followed)
  • Improve platform features and performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these limited cases:

Public Content

Videos, your username, profile picture, and public profile details are visible to other users by design.

Service Providers

We work with trusted partners for hosting (Appwrite), video delivery (Bunny CDN), and payments (Razorpay). They only access data needed to perform their services.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and platform.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted password storage and secure connections. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Delete videos you've uploaded
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Control notification preferences
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@gorkhareels.com.

6. Children's Privacy

GorkhaReels is not intended for children under 13. Users between 13 and 18 should have parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and improve your experience. You can clear these through your browser settings, though some features may not work properly without them.

8. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data, though some information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the platform. Your continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
